Our Story

Founded with a vision to make cybersecurity accessible and effective for businesses of all sizes, Secmetrics has grown to become a trusted partner in digital protection. Based in Gjøvik, Norway, we combine Nordic innovation with global expertise.

Our team of cybersecurity experts is dedicated to developing cutting-edge solutions that address the evolving threat landscape. We believe that robust security should not come at the expense of usability or business efficiency.

From AI-powered threat detection to comprehensive compliance solutions, we're committed to keeping your business secure in an increasingly connected world.
